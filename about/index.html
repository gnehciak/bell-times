Bell Times

About Bell Times

A calm, glanceable clock for the school day: how long is left in the current period, and what comes next.

Bell Times answers one question for Sydney public high schools: how long until the bell? Pick your school and you get a live countdown that ticks every second and adapts to each day's schedule, including split lunches, sport afternoons, assembly days, optional early periods and early finishes.

How it works

Each school's bell schedule is built into the site. When you open your school's page, the countdown reads the current time, finds the period you're in, and shows the time remaining alongside the day's full schedule. It runs entirely in your browser, and nothing is sent anywhere.

Where the times come from

Bell times are taken from each school's official bell-times page on schools.nsw.gov.au. A handful are hand-checked; most are collected automatically, so a school may occasionally be out of date or wrong. Every school page links back to its official source so you can confirm.

Accuracy and disclaimer

Bell Times is unofficial. It's a convenience, not an authority. Always check against your school's official timetable, especially for sport days, exams, and one-off changes. If you spot a mistake, the official source link on each school's page is the place to verify.

Privacy

No accounts, no sign-in, no personal data. The site uses privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics to count page views, nothing that identifies you.
