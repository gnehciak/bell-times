The Northmead Creative and Performing Arts High School Difference

HPGE

Our HPGE program challenges and inspires gifted students through an enriched curriculum and tailored learning experiences designed to develop academic potential, creativity and leadership.

CAPA

CAPA encourages creativity and self-expression in music, drama, dance, and visual arts, helping students build confidence and collaboration through performances and exhibitions.

Personal Growth and Performance

At Northmead CAPA, we support every student’s growth with personalised, inclusive learning that builds skills, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.
