Principal's message

Welcome to Kirrawee High School

Kirrawee High School is a dynamic, inclusive community that delivers a high quality education while ensuring every young person has the opportunity to grow, learn and contribute. At the heart of our work is a commitment to high quality teaching and learning, and a belief that both staff and students should be supported and challenged to be their best selves every day.

Our three Signature Strengths sit at the centre of this commitment:

By fostering resilient learners, we help students set goals, embrace challenges and build confidence in their capacity to improve.

By developing respectful global citizens, we create opportunities for students to engage with diversity, connect with others, and see their place in a broader world.

By encouraging responsible and engaged participation, we support students to take ownership of their learning and contribute positively to school and community life.

As Principal, I am committed to leading a school where staff are empowered to teach at their best, and students are supported to learn at their best. This means setting high expectations, valuing wellbeing, and ensuring that every student is known, challenged and cared for.

Kirrawee High School is not simply defined by outcomes — it is defined by the way we help young people discover their strengths, grow through effort and persistence, and prepare for life beyond school with optimism and purpose.

Christine Campbell, Principal of Kirrawee High School
